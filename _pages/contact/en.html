Get technical assistance, declare a bug or a feature wish

It is always a good idea to first browse the documentation, including the Troubleshooting section, and search the forum. If your request is about a bug, open a new issue in the concerned Github repository ; if you do not know which one it is, use the forum.

If you still need help, start a new topic in the forum to get assistance from the community. Precise which creature you are using, your SD card image or your Pypot version.
