Ergo-Jr: a robot designed for education

Ergo-Jr is a small robotic arm designed for learning computer science. It helps you understand how to build and program robots.



We designed the Ergo-Jr while researchers in the Inria Flowers team. It was created and designed in collaboration with many school teachers to best fit curriculum requirements.

{% include separator.html %}

Why the Ergo-Jr?

3D printings

The structure is entirely 3D printed. The 3D models are available and can be freely modified.

Maker at heart

Accessible, open-source, hackable. The Ergo-Jr is built upon the RaspberryPi board and can be easily combined with Arduino.

STEM

It can be programmed in Scratch and Python. It comes with pedagogical activites designed with school teachers to really fit the curriculum.

The Ergo-Jr is open-source and belongs to the Makers movement. It can be freely modified, adapted, hacked to give birth to new amazing projects.
