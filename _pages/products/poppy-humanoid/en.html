Poppy-Humanoid: an open-source robot open-source for research, education and art

The Poppy project is born from a collaboration between Matthieu Lapeyre and Pierre Rouanet, in the Inria Flowers lab. They were driven by the will to built an open-source, reproductible, and gathering a large community platform. Thanks to their technological expertise they made Poppy Humanoid.

{% include separator.html %}

Why Poppy-Humanoid?

Research

Poppy Humanoid has been designed in a research project aiming at creating a research robotic platform that could be easily reproduced. Its mechanical structure can be quickly modified.

Education

Case study for the engineer schools especially in mecanics, electronics and computer science. Its open-source release let you modify it and transform it into brand new projects.

Art

Its articulated and bio-inspired morphology and its strong aesthetic made it a study object in artistic creation about the study of motion and interaction.

{% include separator.html %}

Poppy Humanoid has been largely diffused. It gave birth to a large and diversed community that created new amazing projects.
