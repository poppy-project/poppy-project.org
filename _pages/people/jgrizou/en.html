Jonathan Grizou

Jonathan Grizou holds an engineer diploma in electrical and computer science from INSA Toulouse, a Msc in Micro and Nano Systems from Toulouse Paul Sabatier University, and a PhD in computer science from INRIA, specialized in artificial intelligence and its application in human-robot interfaces . During his PhD under the supervision of Dr. Pierre-Yves Oudeyer, Jonathan was interested in building smarter, more adaptive, human-machine interfaces with practical applications to brain-computer interaction. He received the "Prix Le Monde de la Recherche Universitaire 2015" for his work, a prize awarded to 5 young French scientists across all fields of science.

Jonathan is also a long time makers with advanced abilities in electronics, mechanics, and programming. He was using professional CAD software by age 12 and built his own 3D printer back in 2011. His practical knowledge of robotics, combined with his expert knowledge in artificial intelligence, provides a set of transversal skills primordial for robotics innovation.
